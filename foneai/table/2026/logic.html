2026 SCORING LOGIC

The Icebraking Points System rewards every classified finisher on a smoothly decaying curve. For 2026 the grid expands to 22 drivers across 11 teams (Cadillac joins), so the same cubic architecture is extended to 22 positions. All bonuses and classification rules are identical to 2025.

Main Race — Formula

P(n) = 76 × [ (23 − n)³ − 1 ] ⁄ 10647
n = finishing position (1–22) · P(1) = 76.00 · P(22) = 0.00 · denominator = 22³ − 1

Classification Rules

  • Only classified finishers score race points; a non-classified driver scores 0 for that event.
  • DNF / DNS / DSQ = 0 race points, regardless of track position at retirement.
  • Classified finishers are ranked 1…k in order, so the last classified driver earns the points for their actual classified position.
  • The main race and sprint are fully independent events.
  • Bonuses are event-specific and awarded independently; the main-race fastest-lap bonus is independent of classification.

Constructor Points

A constructor's total is the sum of both its drivers' points across every session and bonus. There were no mid-season driver changes in 2026, so each of the 11 teams' total is simply the combined score of its two drivers.

What Changed from 2025

  • Drivers: 20 → 22; constructors: 10 → 11 (Cadillac added).
  • Main-race maximum: 56 → 76; denominator 7999 → 10647 (22³ − 1).
  • Sprint maximum stays 12, on the same extended curve.
  • Maximum possible single-weekend haul: 71.50 → 91.50 points.
