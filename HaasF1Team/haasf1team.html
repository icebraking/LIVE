Haas F1 Team

Haas F1 Team, the first American-led entrant since 1986, was founded in 2014 by NASCAR owner Gene Haas and debuted in 2016 with the VF-16 chassis, utilizing Ferrari power units and customer components for cost efficiency. Based in Kannapolis, North Carolina, Haas achieved eighth in Constructors' in its rookie year, with Romain Grosjean's podium at Bahrain.
